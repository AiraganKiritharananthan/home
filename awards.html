Introduction-

On this webpage you will find the various awards I have won.

Most of these awards are from school or various extracurricular

activites, such as graduation or music.

Source: "yellow and white trophy", by Giorgio Trovato, January 5, 2020

Graduation Award-

During my grade 6 graduation, I won the Social Studies award, this was a memorable

event because it was my first graduation. During the graduation my classmates and

I had a lot of fun and it was a really good experience overall.

MSEO Award-

In grade 7 my school, Sir Ernest MacMillan has a event called Science Olympics

which was hosted by W.L. Mackenzie. I was registered under the coding section

and came out first out of all of the schools that participated it was really fun

and a great experience, and I would do it again.

Soccer Award-

A few years ago, I won this award for placing 3rd in the 9th division for my

soccer league. Although I don't play soccer anymore I still am proud that I

got to play with such amazing people. This was one of the first sports

awards I recieved so it is really special to me.

Music Award-

I got this award for participating in a concert at Midi Melodies, I was

playing the keyboard (electric piano) with many people. Even if I had a

small part of the concert it was really fun that I got to play.
